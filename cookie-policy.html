Cookie Policy

Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies in the context of our services related to business automation powered by artificial intelligence (AI). By continuing to use our website and digital platforms, you agree to the use of cookies as outlined below.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow us to recognize your browser and gather information to enhance your experience, especially in relation to AI-based automation services.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for core site functionality and enable features related to secure login, system performance, and session tracking for our AI automation tools. Without these, the site may not operate properly.

2. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies help remember user preferences and configuration settings specific to our business automation solutions. These enhance usability and help personalize your journey.

3. Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with our AI-driven automation services, which pages are most used, and how our system tools perform. This data helps us continuously improve service efficiency.

4. Performance & Optimization Cookies

These cookies track usage patterns and system responsiveness to optimize AI workflows and improve backend efficiency across our automation modules.

How We Use Cookie Data

1. Improving AI-Powered Automation Tools

Collected cookie data helps refine and adapt our AI models, ensuring smoother process automation, better task prediction, and smart recommendation delivery.

2. User Behavior Analytics

Analyzing cookie data allows us to evaluate how users interact with business automation interfaces and tailor content, dashboards, and AI-powered functions accordingly.

3. Security and System Stability

Some cookies are used for security monitoring and to prevent misuse of our AI automation environment. Others ensure load balancing and consistent performance during high-traffic periods.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential or functional cookies may limit your ability to use some features of our AI-driven automation platform effectively.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use trusted third-party tools that place cookies to support analytics, service delivery, or real-time automation insights. These cookies are managed in compliance with applicable privacy standards and only serve operational needs related to business automation.

Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology or service functionality. Updates will be published on this page. Continued use of the site implies acceptance of the most recent version.
